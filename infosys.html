About the job.

Job Title: Software Engineering IC3

Company: Infosys

Location: On-site.

Duration:Permanent.

Employment Opportunity: Full time based.

Job Description: We are looking for a full stack Software Engineer to join us to deliver innovative and world-class solutions. This position is a unique opportunity to be part of an outstanding team, building one of the most exciting security products in the cyber security space from the ground up. We are a diverse, multicultural, and inclusive team and this is reflected in everything we do. Our team has a sense of accountability and provides its members with many opportunities for learning and career growth.

Responsibilities:
  • Work with engineers, program managers, partner teams and end customers to deliver a set of features with the right overall design and architecture.
  • Write a clean well-thought-out code with an emphasis on quality, performance, simplicity, durability, scalability, and maintainability.
  • Participate in periodic on-call rotations to handle service incidents, incident postmortem etc.
  • Be committed and accountable to delivering the best experience for our customers.

We are seeking an experienced Software Engineer with 1 to 3 years of industry experience to join our growing team.You will be responsible for applying advanced analytics and machine learning techniques to extract valuable insights from complex datasets. You will play a key role in shaping data-driven strategies and contributing to the development of innovative solutions.

  1. Conduct end-to-end data analysis, from data collection and pre-processing to model development and deployment.
  2. Help create a diverse and inclusive culture where everyone can bring their full and authentic self and where we do our best work as a result.
  3. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand business requirements and translate them into solutions.
  4. Stay updated with the latest advancements in technologies.
About our Hiring Partner:

We intend to provide the full suite of IT/ITeS Services to various customers around automation, application development and management, IT Support Centers managing the whole gamut of IT Infrastructure and environment, Cloud Computing, Cyber Security, Artificial intelligence, Data Analytics, managing various finance/revenue/HR/Payroll/Procurement functions in a highly automated manner.

We intend to deliver these services across industry sectors such as retail, consumer packaged goods, edutech, e-commerce, hi-tech, engineering R&D, manufacturing, automotive and travel/ transportation/ hospitality.
